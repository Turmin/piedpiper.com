Welcome Our New Employee: Carla Walton!

By Jared Dunn

Hello readers!

This is probably our biggest announcement to date: We have a new employee, who is also a woman: Carla Walton!

Carla was hired because she was the best female candidate for the job, not because she was a woman—although being a woman made her uniquely qualified and will allow her to bring her unique female experiences and perspective to building Pied Piper’s streaming capabilities with vp9 and p2p, as a female programmer who happens to be a woman.

So far no “haters” have come forward and done any “hating” about this hire, which should in no way be controversial, but if any are thinking about it, I’ll just say this: Carla will be performing her duties just as well as a man could…maybe even better, who knows???
